About US

Welcome to PRAS BLOOMS

At PRAS BLOOMS, we believe that every bouquet tells a story. Our passion for flowers drives us to create beautiful, handcrafted arrangements that bring joy, love, and beauty to any occasion.

Our Story

PRAS BLOOMS was founded in 2020 by Prasuma Bista, a lifelong lover of flowers with a background in horticulture and design. What began as a small, local flower stand has blossomed into a beloved community floral shop known for its exceptional quality and personalized service.

Committment to sustainability

At PRAS BLOOMS, We're dedicated to using sustainable methods. We try to lessen our carbon footprint, utilize recyclable packaging, and give priority to eco-friendly sourcing. Our mission is to care for the earth and infuse it with beauty.
